Hello, we are...

Impact.

We are part of this generation facing one of the biggest challenges Humanity has known, the Environmental Crisis.

We are convinced that it is absolutely necessary to act for future generations. In the last few years, something has gone wrong with the way we consume: more and more products, manufactured far away from home, with poor quality and disastrous consequences for our planet. Many of us want to change our way of consuming. To consume better, to consume less, to consume more responsibly, without making any compromise on the quality of our daily life products.

But sometimes it is difficult to sort out all these labels, so most of the time, we go back to the unsustainable products we know.

Yet real alternatives to mass consumption exist: sustainable & ethical products at fair prices.

But how to convince as many people as possible to replace their daily life products with more sustainable ones ?

That's when we decided to start Impact.

What we want is for you to avoid searching for hours to find sustainable products that fit your needs.

To make it easier for you, we've put together a selection of eco-friendly alternatives to everyday products. Our role is to find beautiful and environmentally friendly products that are ethically manufactured. So that you can make the most responsible choice possible according to your environmental sensitivity, we give you all the information at our disposal on the products we select.

What you will find here are products made by committed merchants who share our environmental and social convictions.

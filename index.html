Impact. Your products Our Universe

Hello, we are...

Impact.

We are part of this generation that has to face one of the biggest challenges Humanity has known, the Environmental Crisis.

We are convinced that it is absolutely necessary to act for future generations. In the last few years, something has gone wrong with the way we consume: more and more products, manufactured far away from home, with poor quality and disastrous consequences for our planet. Many of us want to change our way of consuming. To consume better, to consume less, to consume more responsibly, without making any compromise on the quality of our daily life products.

But sometimes it is difficult to sort out all these labels, so most of the time, we go back to the unsustainable products we know.

Yet real alternatives to mass consumption exist: sustainable & ethical products at fair prices.

So we asked ourselves how to convince as many people as possible to replace their daily life products with more sustainable ones.

That's when we decided to start Impact.

What we want is for you to avoid searching for hours to find sustainable products that fit your needs

Pour vous faciliter la tâche, on a concocté une sélection d’alternatives écolo à des produits de la vie quotidienne. Notre rôle c’est de dénicher des beaux produits respectueux de l’environnement fabriqués de manière éthique près de chez vous. Pour que vous puissiez faire le choix le plus responsable possible selon votre sensibilité environnementale on vous donne toutes les informations à notre disposition sur les produits que nous sélectionnons.

Ce que vous trouverez chez nous, c’est des produits faits par des commerçants engagés qui partagent nos convictions environnementales et sociales.
